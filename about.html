About

Circible is a college project for a communincations leadership class.

This project aims to spread the word of God by servicing 24 hour bible verses daily.

Verses updated by me. Each verse applies what is going on in the world in the given day.

Updated: 10pm-12pm - Central Time.

Translation: New International Version (NIV).
